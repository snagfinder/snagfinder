It's time to get your snag on!

🌭

Frustrated with the fact that there is no publicly available centralised database of upcoming Bunnings sausage sizzles, Kate and some of her friends have en-devour-ed to create one.

The purpose of this unofficial website is to highlight the incredible community organisations and lead punters to retrieve a succulent snag. It is a community run page, so we rely on your sightings to keep the map alive!

Whatever your sauce, let's support our brilliant local groups!

Note: This is a community fan project and is not officially affiliated with Bunnings Warehouse.
